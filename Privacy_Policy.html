Our commitment to protecting your personal information

Compliance and Privacy Commitment

At HubTechResearch, we take the privacy and security of our users and clients seriously. We are committed to ensuring that your personal data is handled with the utmost care and in full compliance with applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the CAN-SPAM Act.

1. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Compliance

As a company that serves clients within the European Union (EU) and beyond, HubTechResearch adheres to the GDPR to ensure the responsible and transparent handling of personal data. Below are the key points of our GDPR compliance:

Data Collection and Use

We collect only the personal data necessary to provide our services (e.g., names, contact information, company names) and will always explain how and why we collect this information.

User Consent

We obtain explicit consent before collecting personal data through forms or cookies. Consent is freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous. You have the right to withdraw consent at any time.

Data Subject Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. If you would like to exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@hubtechresearch.com.

Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure storage, and strict access controls.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, after which it will be securely deleted.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and for analytics purposes. You can control your cookie preferences by adjusting your browser settings or using our Cookie Consent tool, which allows you to accept or reject non-essential cookies.

2. CAN-SPAM Act Compliance

We are fully committed to complying with the CAN-SPAM Act, which governs the sending of commercial emails to recipients in the United States. Here's how we adhere to the law:

Unsubscribe Option

Every marketing email sent by HubTechResearch includes a clear and easy-to-find unsubscribe link, allowing you to opt out of receiving further communications at any time.

Honest and Transparent Subject Lines

We ensure that the subject lines of our emails are truthful and accurately represent the content of the message.

Physical Address

As required by the CAN-SPAM Act, all of our commercial emails contain a valid physical mailing address for HubTechResearch. This provides transparency and ensures compliance with the law.

Clear Sender Information

We provide clear and identifiable sender information in every commercial email, so recipients can easily recognize who the email is from.
