Privacy Policy

Compliance and Privacy Commitment:

At HubTechResearch, we take the privacy and security of our users and clients seriously. We are committed to ensuring that your personal data is handled with the utmost care and in full compliance with applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the CAN-SPAM Act.

1. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Compliance

As a company that serves clients within the European Union (EU) and beyond, HubTechResearch adheres to the GDPR to ensure the responsible and transparent handling of personal data. Below are the key points of our GDPR compliance:

Data Collection and Use: We collect only the personal data necessary to provide our services (e.g., names, contact information, company names) and will always explain how and why we collect this information.

User Consent: We obtain explicit consent before collecting personal data through forms or cookies. Consent is freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous. You have the right to withdraw consent at any time.

Data Subject Rights: Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. If you would like to exercise these rights, please contact us info@hubtechresearch.com.

Data Security: We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure storage, and strict access controls.

Data Retention: We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, after which it will be securely deleted.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and for analytics purposes. You can control your cookie preferences by adjusting your browser settings or using our Cookie Consent tool, which allows you to accept or reject non-essential cookies.

2. CAN-SPAM Act Compliance

We are fully committed to complying with the CAN-SPAM Act, which governs the sending of commercial emails to recipients in the United States. Here's how we adhere to the law:

Unsubscribe Option: Every marketing email sent by HubTechResearch includes a clear and easy-to-find unsubscribe link, allowing you to opt out of receiving further communications at any time.

Honest and Transparent Subject Lines: We ensure that the subject lines of our emails are truthful and accurately represent the content of the message.

Physical Address: As required by the CAN-SPAM Act, all of our commercial emails contain a valid physical mailing address for HubTechResearch. This provides transparency and ensures compliance with the law.

Clear Sender Information: We provide clear and identifiable sender information in every commercial email, so recipients can easily recognize who the email is from.

3. Your Privacy Choices

We respect your privacy and provide several ways to manage your data preferences:

Opt-Out of Marketing Communications: You can easily unsubscribe from our marketing emails by clicking the unsubscribe link found at the bottom of all our emails. Alternatively, you can contact us directly at unsubscribe@hubtechresearch.com.

Access to Your Personal Data: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. Please contact us at info@hubtechresearch.com to make such requests.

Data Deletion: If you wish to have your personal data deleted, please send us a request at info@hubtechresearch.com, and we will take action in accordance with your wishes, subject to legal retention requirements.

Access and Updates: We may update this compliance section from time to time to reflect changes in the law or our practices. We recommend that you review it periodically.

At HubTechResearch, we are committed to maintaining a secure environment for your personal data. We continuously review and update our security measures to adapt to the evolving landscape of cyber threats and to ensure that your privacy is safeguarded in accordance with global data protection standards, including GDPR.

If you have any questions regarding our security practices or would like more information on how we protect your data, please feel free to contact us at info@hubtechresearch.com.
