About HubTechResearch

Driving insight-driven growth for B2B teams through data and strategy

Our Story

Founded in 2015, HubTechResearch began as a small team of technology enthusiasts passionate about bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and practical business applications.

What started as a technology blog quickly evolved into a full-service research and marketing firm as we recognized the growing need for data-driven marketing strategies in the tech sector.

Today, we serve clients across 25 countries, helping them leverage technology insights to create impactful marketing campaigns.

Our Mission

To guide B2B teams toward smarter & insight driven growth.

We help businesses uncover high intent opportunities, align sales and marketing, and drive meaningful conversations with the right prospects all through precise, actionable data and a strategic partnership approach.

Whether you're scaling outreach, refining targeting or building pipeline confidence, we're here to support your journey with intelligence that moves the needle.
